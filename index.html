How can we prevent

Prevent aims to challenge extremist ideologies and prevent people from being radicalised and involved in terrorism or supporting terrorist activities.

    • Signs

    • Isolating from friends and relatives.
    • Spending a lot of time online or on their phone, and being secretive about what they are doing.
    • Increased level of anger, disrespectful attitude and sudden aggresiveness.
    • Having several profiles on social media, using their own name or aliases to share extreme views
    • Support or sympathy for groups with extreme political or religious views, who encourage illegal or violent action
    • Accessing extremist literature or contacting those wanting to recruit to extremist groups.
    • justifying the use of violence to solve societal issues.
    • altering their style of dress or appearance to accord with an extremist group.
    • using the dark web or anonymous browsing to hide online activity
    • reading, posting, watching, or sharing hateful or extreme content and links
